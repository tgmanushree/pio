About Us

As healed healers, we have been working for more than ten years conducting counselling and training sessions to help people triumph in various aspects of life. As the next phase, to cater to the greater need of society, we have teamed under the banner of PIO counselling and training center with a common vision and mission to eradicate needless worries in the hope of invoking peace.

Founder's Profile

Arthi Sujai is the founder of PIO Counseling and Training Services. With over a decade of experience in counselling and training, she is passionate about helping individuals overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.

Arthi holds a Master's degree in Psychology and has received numerous accolades for her contributions to the field of mental health.

NOTE FROM FOUNDER

Throughout my extensive career, my focus has been on revitalizing marital relationships, empowering individuals on journeys of self-improvement, and strengthening parent-child bonds. I bring a wealth of expertise in evidence-based therapies such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT), and Mindfulness-Based CBT, tailoring interventions to address negative thought patterns and foster positive behavioral change. Additionally, my proficiency extends to Imago Couple Therapy, enhancing communication skills, and employing strategies from Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) for mindful living. Adept in conflict management, I help individuals and families navigate challenges to build healthier, more fulfilling relationships. With a commitment to maintaining confidentiality and utilizing strong case formulation skills, I create a safe and supportive environment. My passion lies in fostering well-being, promoting positive connections, and making a meaningful impact on the lives of those I serve. Let's connect and embark on a journey towards positive transformation.
